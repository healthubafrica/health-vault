Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026 · Health-Hub Africa® | MyHealth Vault+™

1. Overview

Health-Hub Africa® ("HHA", "we", "us") operates MyHealth Vault+™ (the "Platform"), accessible at www.myvaultplus.com and portal.myvaultplus.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and health information when you use the Platform.

By creating an account and using MyHealth Vault+™, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Registration Information:

When you create an account, we collect your full name, date of birth, gender, phone number, email address, city/address, and emergency contact details.

Health Information (Protected Health Information — PHI):

When you use clinical services, we collect and store health-related information including medical history, diagnoses, prescriptions, lab results, imaging reports, consultation notes, allergies, and chronic conditions.

Service Usage Data:

We collect information about how you use the Platform including appointment bookings, Expert Review™ submissions, DispatchCare™ requests, subscription activity, and login sessions.

Device & Technical Data:

We may collect your IP address, browser type, device type, and general location data (used for DispatchCare™ dispatch purposes).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your MyHealth Vault+™ account
  • To provide and coordinate healthcare services on your behalf
  • To display your health records within your secure portal
  • To process payments for paid services and subscriptions
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, results alerts, and Expert Review™ status updates via email, SMS, and WhatsApp
  • To coordinate emergency dispatch through DispatchCare™
  • To improve and develop the MyHealth Vault+™ platform
  • To comply with applicable health data and privacy regulations

4. Your Data is Not on Your Device

MyHealth Vault+™ is designed with a zero-PHI-on-device architecture. Your clinical records are never stored, cached, or persisted on your phone or computer. All health data is held on secure, access-controlled servers and delivered only to your authenticated, live session.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your individually identifiable health information with employers, third-party advertisers, or external parties without your explicit consent, except:

  • When required to coordinate healthcare services you have requested (e.g., specialist assignment for Expert Review™)
  • When required by applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • When you have provided explicit written consent for a specific sharing purpose

For Corporate/HMO plans, only aggregated and fully de-identified usage data is shared with the contracting organisation. Individual patient records are never disclosed to employers or HMO administrators.

6. Data Security

MyHealth Vault+™ employs industry-standard security practices including end-to-end encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), server-side encryption at rest, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) on all data endpoints, secure signed URLs for document access (with automatic expiry), short-lived JWT authentication tokens, and regular security audits.

7. Data Retention

We retain your health records for as long as your account is active and as required by applicable clinical data retention regulations. You may request deletion of non-clinical account data at any time by contacting support@healthubafrica.com. Clinical records may be subject to minimum retention periods under Nigerian health regulations.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, correct inaccurate personal information, request deletion of non-clinical account data, opt out of non-essential communications, and request a copy of your clinical records in a structured digital format.

Contact us at: support@healthubafrica.com

9. Cookies

www.myvaultplus.com uses essential cookies to maintain your session and support platform functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via email and posted on this page. Continued use of the Platform following notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact — Privacy Matters

For privacy-related enquiries or to exercise your rights:

support@healthubafrica.com
