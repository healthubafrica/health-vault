DispatchCare™

Emergency Help. One Tap.

In a medical emergency, seconds matter. DispatchCare™ is the emergency dispatch module inside your MyHealth Vault+™ portal — accessible from every screen, in every session, with one tap.

How DispatchCare™ Works

From tap to dispatch in seconds

1

One Tap

Hit the red DispatchCare™ emergency button. It’s always visible on your dashboard and accessible from any screen.

2

Your Location is Detected

DispatchCare™ auto-detects your location using your device’s GPS. If unavailable, enter your address manually.

3

Select Your Emergency Type

Choose what’s happening — from Chest Pain to Stroke Symptoms — so the right response is coordinated.

4

Dispatch Now

Your case is instantly sent to the HHA operations team. A case ID is generated (DC-YYYY-000001). You receive immediate SMS and WhatsApp confirmation.

5

We Call You

The HHA team contacts you. Your case status updates in real time. Call and WhatsApp support remain visible throughout.
