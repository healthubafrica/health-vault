**Page Headline:**
# Health-Hub Expert Review™
## A Second Opinion Changes Everything.

**Intro:**
You've received a diagnosis. A treatment has been recommended. But something doesn't sit right. Or perhaps the path ahead isn't clear. Health-Hub Expert Review™ gives you access to qualified, independent clinical expertise — reviewed, documented, and delivered securely through your MyHealth Vault+™ portal.

---

## What is Expert Review™?

Expert Review™ is Health-Hub Africa's premium digital second opinion and multi-specialist case review service. It is not a general enquiry or chat with a doctor. It is a structured clinical review, conducted by verified specialists, resulting in a written report that becomes a permanent, downloadable record in your Vault.

---

## What Can Be Reviewed?

- **Second Opinions** — Get an independent view on any diagnosis before making a treatment decision
- **Multi-Specialist Panels** — Complex cases reviewed by a panel of specialists across relevant fields
- **Lab & Imaging Reviews** — Specialist interpretation of blood work, scans, MRIs, X-rays, and pathology
- **Post-Discharge Reviews** — Review your discharge summary, plan, and medications with a specialist
- **Treatment Plan Reviews** — Validate a proposed treatment approach before you commit
- **Medication Reviews** — Specialist review of your current or prescribed medications
- **Specialist Referral Reviews** — Review a referral letter or recommendation before proceeding

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Submit Your Case**
Log in to your portal at portal.myvaultplus.com. Select Expert Review™ and complete the intake form: review type, clinical concern, symptoms, diagnosis, and urgency.

**Step 2 — Upload Your Documents**
Attach any relevant documents: lab results, imaging reports, discharge summaries, prescriptions, referral notes, prior records. Files up to 20MB per document.

**Step 3 — HHA Reviews Your Case**
The Health-Hub Africa® clinical coordination team reviews your intake, confirms your documents, checks your plan entitlement, and assigns the right specialist or panel.

**Step 4 — Specialist Review in Progress**
Your case is reviewed by a verified specialist in the relevant clinical field. You can track every status update in real time inside your portal.

**Step 5 — Receive Your Report**
Your final specialist report is generated, approved, and delivered securely to your MyHealth Vault+™ portal. Download it as a PDF. Share it with your treating physician.

---

# 8. FAQ PAGE

**Page Headline:**
# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Getting Started

**What is MyHealth Vault+™?**
MyHealth Vault+™ is a secure digital health portal built by Health-Hub Africa®. It gives you a single, organised place to access your health records, book care services, request emergency dispatch, and get specialist second opinions.

**Is MyHealth Vault+™ free to use?**
Yes. Creating a MyHealth Vault+™ account is completely free. You'll have access to your portal, limited record viewing, and core services on a pay-per-use basis. Paid subscription plans unlock expanded access and included services.

**How do I create an account?**
Visit portal.myvaultplus.com/register and complete the registration form. You'll need your name, date of birth, phone number, email address, and an emergency contact. Your unique Patient ID is generated instantly.

**How long does registration take?**
Under 3 minutes for most patients.

**What is my Patient ID?**
Your Patient ID is a unique identifier in the format HHA-XXX-YYMM-XXXX (e.g. HHA-LAG-2605-0004). It's generated automatically when you register and appears on your dashboard.

---

## Your Account & Records

**Where are my health records stored?**
Your records are stored securely on protected clinical servers — not on your phone or computer. You can view and download them through your portal at any time. No clinical data is ever cached locally on your device.

**Can I download my medical records?**
Yes. You can download individual records, lab results, and reports as PDF files from your portal.

**Can I update my personal and medical information?**
Yes. You can edit your personal information, medical details (allergies, conditions, medications, blood group), and emergency contact from your Profile screen inside the portal.

**Is my data shared with anyone?**
Your clinical data is strictly patient-owned. It is never shared with employers, third-party advertisers, or external parties without your explicit consent. For Corporate/HMO plans, only aggregated and de-identified usage data is available to the organisation.

---

## Services

**How do I book a TeleCare™ consultation?**
Log in to your portal, select Book a Service from the dashboard or navigation, choose TeleCare™, pick a date and time slot, add a reason for visit, and confirm. You'll receive a confirmation by email and SMS.

**What is DispatchCare™?**
DispatchCare™ is the emergency medical dispatch service inside your portal. One tap sends your location and case details to the Health-Hub Africa® operations team for immediate dispatch coordination. It is accessible from every screen in the portal.

**What is Expert Review™?**
Expert Review™ is Health-Hub Africa's specialist second opinion service. You can submit a clinical case — including documents, imaging, and lab results — for review by a qualified specialist or multi-specialist panel. You receive a written specialist report, stored permanently in your Vault.

**What documents can I upload for Expert Review™?**
Lab results, imaging and scan reports, discharge summaries, prescriptions, referral letters, and prior medical records. Maximum 20MB per file.

---

## Plans & Billing

**What plans are available?**
Free, Basic, Mid-Level, Gold, and Corporate/HMO. Plans differ in service access levels, Expert Review™ credits, DispatchCare™ routing priority, and included services. See the full comparison at myvaultplus.com/plans.

**How do I upgrade my plan?**
Log in to your portal, go to Subscriptions & Billing, select your preferred plan, and complete payment. Your plan activates as soon as your payment is confirmed.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Payments are processed via Paystack (primary) and Flutterwave. Card, bank transfer, and USSD options are available.

**Can I download invoices?**
Yes. All invoices and payment receipts are available as PDF downloads from your Subscriptions & Billing screen.

---

## Support

**How do I get support?**
You can reach the Health-Hub Africa® support team via WhatsApp, phone call, or support ticket — all accessible from within your portal and from the Contact page on this website.

**What if I have a medical emergency right now?**
Use the DispatchCare™ button inside your portal immediately. If you're not logged in or can't access the portal, call the HHA Emergency Line directly or contact emergency services in your location.
