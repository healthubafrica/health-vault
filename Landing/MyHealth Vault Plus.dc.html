— Security & Trust

Your health data is a vault. Treated like one.

Zero PHI on Your Device

Your clinical records are never stored, cached, or saved locally on your phone or computer. All data lives securely on protected servers and is delivered only to your authenticated session.

End-to-End Protected

Every request passes through end-to-end encryption. All connections secured via HTTPS/TLS. No exceptions.

Clinically Governed

Built on a FHIR R4-compliant clinical infrastructure governed by Health-Hub Africa® clinical standards. Your records are accurate, structured, and auditable.
