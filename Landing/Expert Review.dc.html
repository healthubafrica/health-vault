Expert Review™

A Second Opinion Changes Everything.

You’ve received a diagnosis. A treatment has been recommended. But something doesn’t sit right. Health-Hub Expert Review™ gives you access to qualified, independent clinical expertise — reviewed, documented, and delivered securely through your MyHealth Vault+™ portal.

What is Expert Review™

A structured clinical review — not a chat with a doctor.

Expert Review™ is Health-Hub Africa's premium digital second opinion and multi-specialist case review service. It is conducted by verified specialists, resulting in a written report that becomes a permanent, downloadable record in your Vault.

What Can Be Reviewed

Seven kinds of expert review

Second Opinions

Get an independent view on any diagnosis before making a treatment decision

Multi-Specialist Panels

Complex cases reviewed by a panel of specialists across relevant fields

Lab & Imaging Reviews

Specialist interpretation of blood work, scans, MRIs, X-rays, and pathology

Post-Discharge Reviews

Review your discharge summary, plan, and medications with a specialist

Treatment Plan Reviews

Validate a proposed treatment approach before you commit

Medication Reviews

Specialist review of your current or prescribed medications

Specialist Referral Reviews

Review a referral letter or recommendation before proceeding

How It Works

Five steps, fully tracked

1

Submit Your Case

Log in, select Expert Review™ and complete the intake: review type, clinical concern, symptoms, diagnosis, and urgency.

2

Upload Your Documents

Attach lab results, imaging reports, discharge summaries, prescriptions, referral notes, prior records. Up to 20MB per document.

3

HHA Reviews Your Case

The clinical coordination team reviews your intake, confirms documents, checks plan entitlement, and assigns the right specialist or panel.

4

Specialist Review in Progress

Your case is reviewed by a verified specialist in the relevant field. Track every status update in real time inside your portal.

5

Receive Your Report

Your final specialist report is generated, approved, and delivered securely to your Vault. Download as PDF. Share with your physician.

Important Note

All Expert Review™ final reports include the following disclaimer: "This Expert Review is based on the medical information, documents, and history provided by the patient. It is intended to support clinical decision-making and does not replace emergency care, physical examination, or ongoing management by the patient's treating physician. If symptoms worsen or become urgent, the patient should seek immediate medical attention."
