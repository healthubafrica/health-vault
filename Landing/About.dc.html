About MyHealth Vault+™

The patient-facing digital health portal built by Health-Hub Africa® — a secure, intelligent, and personalised digital home for healthcare in Africa.

What is MyHealth Vault+™

MyHealth Vault+™ is a layered digital health platform that transforms clinical infrastructure into a consumer-grade patient experience — giving every patient in Africa a secure, intelligent, and personalised digital home for their healthcare.

It is not a basic health app. It is not a static medical record viewer. It is a living, integrated health ecosystem: where records, care bookings, emergency dispatch, specialist reviews, and subscription health services converge in a single, beautifully simple portal.

Our Guiding Principle

Simple interface → Fast request → Clear response → Secure record → Continuous care.

The Platform

Three integrated layers

01

MyHealth Vault+™ UI

The patient-facing web application. Mobile-first. Designed for Africa’s connectivity realities.

02

HHA Middleware

All business logic, service orchestration, subscriptions, payments, and audit. Your data never touches the frontend directly.

03

Clinical Infrastructure

The secure clinical system of record. FHIR R4-compliant. Accessed only through the middleware. No direct database access. Ever.

Strategic Pillars

What it means for you

Secure

No clinical data on your device. All data protected end-to-end.

Scalable

Serves individual free patients through to large corporate and HMO groups.

Service-Led

Six branded care services plus Expert Review™ and NeuroFlex™ from one portal.

Intelligence-Ready

Future AI layers (STRIDE™, HPACS™, EFCE™) connect without rebuilding the patient interface.

The Vision

To give every patient in Africa a secure, intelligent, and personalised digital home for their healthcare — where records, services, emergency care, and specialist second opinions are always within reach.
