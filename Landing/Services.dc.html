TeleCare™

Remote Healthcare, Delivered to You

Consult with qualified healthcare providers via video or phone — from your home, office, or anywhere in Nigeria. TeleCare™ eliminates the need to travel for routine consultations and follow-ups.

Who it's for: All plan tiers (pay-per-use on Free; included or expanded on paid plans)

What's included
Video and phone consultations with licensed providers
Follow-up booking within the same session
Consultation notes added to your MyHealth Vault+™ record
Prescription requests processed through your Vault

DispatchCare™

Emergency Medical Dispatch. One Tap.

DispatchCare™ is your emergency lifeline. In a medical emergency, one tap from any screen in your portal sends your location, case details, and an alert to the Health-Hub Africa® operations team for immediate dispatch coordination.

Who it's for: All patients (Priority routing on Gold and Corporate plans)

What's included
One-tap emergency button accessible from every portal screen
Auto-detects your location via device GPS (manual fallback)
Emergency type selection: Chest Pain, Accident, Stroke & more
Instant case ID (DC-YYYY-000001), SMS & WhatsApp confirmation
HHA emergency call line always visible
Learn More About DispatchCare™
